Mi Hermoso Paìs

"Mi Hermoso Paìs" is a poetic meditation on the resilience of the human spirit. Its imagery invites readers to reflect on their own journeys of hope and aspiration, while its personification of "Esperanza" transforms an abstract ideal into a relatable companion. The poem is both a celebration and a challenge—a reminder that, in the pursuit of hope, the journey itself becomes the reward.

Mi Hermoso Paìs

Pampas de renombre,
Infinitas de esperanza,
Mágica alabanza,
Busquemos la pampa de Constanza.

Esperanza niña linda,
Pálida y melancólica,
Dímelo todo esperanza,
Sea oportuna tu venganza.

Te busco te busco,
montañas de montañas,
profundo del profundo mar y mas,
Mírame, dame algo, esperanzas.

Longevo y Gigantesco,
Mi hermoso pais de esperanzas,
Esperanzas hoy camine,
esperanzas mías esperanzas tuyas divisé.
