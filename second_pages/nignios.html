Nocturnal Echoes

"Luces parpadeantes" explores the juxtaposition between light and darkness, childhood innocence and the passage of time. The repeated references to "luces" (lights) suggest a vibrant, lively atmosphere that contrasts with the underlying themes of loss and transformation.
