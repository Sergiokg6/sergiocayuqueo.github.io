Oda al Bosque
Los mil señores,
Con mil años,
Y mil de miles de millones,
De sombras por doquier.

Silenciosos y non silencio,
De vidas y de non vida,
De robles y non roble,
Leñadores y non leñador.

Es mi protector,
Del prójimo acorazado,
De miles de chips de los miles de señores,
Atormentado de corrientes.

Y suben a las nubes,
Y de las nubes se devuelven,
Del paisaje más lluvioso,
Un Leviatán más natural.

Señores de los rayos,
Y señores del oscuro,
De la casa de los osos,
Y de los osos de los humanos que duermen sin pesar por los miles de mil años en Milán.

Los pasillos Carmesí,

De los congelados en el día de ayer y hoy y mañana y siempre por el infinito Carmesí.

donde los sonidos retumban sin razón,
hay un reloj infinito,
marcando las almas infinitas.

Transitorio sello estampado poseía,

Es babel ya sin aliento.

En el rincón del espacio exterior,
flota un libro --no, una galaxia Estalactita--,
entre polvo estelar y sueños fusilados,

En la máquina de tempestad,
en el simulacro divino del mismo creador,

es el silencio el que juzgará.

La bóveda del quantum de silencio,
transciende la galaxia,
pulveriza fronteras,
donde lo magno es leve y lo efímero más, más eterno.

Atornillado Vagabundo
Me comunicaron,
Extraoficialmente, que
Esto de la innovación tiene horas,
Fue un día oscuro y más oscuro para los que brillaron.

Era el día del año nuevo,
Con todo nuevo,
Personas nuevas, calles nuevas,
Un café nuevo, todo tan nuevo, tan igual.

Las queridísimas oficinas del lugar,
El paseo Ahumada
La gente más ahumada
Del asado ahumado, ya no queda nada

Estaba todo cerrado,
Brillantes eran oscuros,
Todo está por el suelo, mi mirada, mi sonrisa,
Mis esperanzas, la basura.

Es el ajetreo de la calle,
La dinámica de los hechos,
¿Quién te ha confabulado?,
Hermoso árbol mío,
Estructura arquitectónica,
Aquella mañana, ponderaba,
En llegar y la puerta abierta
En llegar y dejar pasar el tiempo
De innovar sin pensar, yo pensaba
Que se trataba de actores orbitales,
Pero no, esa mañana las puertas estaban cerradas,
Más que cerradas, atornilladas,
Es el vagabundo.

no había eruditos,

TEAM & VISION
I entered the creative world as a Poet, to shape and evoke emotions through words. The ‘Poetry Evolution’ journey was ambitious, stretching across weeks of intense reflection. Throughout this journey, I worked hand in hand with fellow writers, reflecting on life’s meaning and experimenting with various literary styles.
ARTISTIC OBJECTIVES
“We capture the essence of life, transforming thought into verse.”

The goal was to create a collection of verses that connect deeply with the reader’s heart, bringing to light the profound moments of human existence. Our target was to evoke emotions, stir memories, and offer glimpses of the soul for those in search of profound introspection.
METHODOLOGY
Based on our shared passion for writing, we decided to focus on the essence of personalization in our poetry. We delved into the depths of individuality, crafting poems that speak directly to the heart of the reader. We categorized this as:
  • Emotion-Based Personalization
  • Reflection of the Reader’s Journey
  • Tailored Experience of Verse
Let us now explore these poetic pathways, where words flow with meaning, shaping the future of the poetic landscape.
EXPLORATION PHASE
  • Crafting Poetic Personas
    Through deep reflection and inspiration, I created characters within my poems—imaginary souls that resonate with readers.
    Luna / The Dreamer
    ‍‍
    Luna is a vision of the night, dancing in the moonlight, forever seeking new horizons through poetry. She writes of places she may never visit but yearns for.
    Max / The Wanderer
    ‍‍
    Max is a traveler of time, and each of his verses is an exploration of different worlds, unraveling experiences with each step.
    Ivy / The Silent Thinker
    ‍‍
    Ivy’s words come from quiet corners, reflecting deep thoughts on nature and existence. She speaks the language of the heart.
  • Mapping Poetic Journeys
    Just as one embarks on an odyssey, the poetic journey begins in silence, grows through the rhythm of the verse, and ends in contemplation. My poems explore each phase, revealing emotions and experiences woven within. These journeys are more than words; they are the path the reader takes to find themselves.
    • I identified the raw emotions that lead to poetic creation.
    • I explored the impact of silence and sound in crafting meaningful verses.
    • I connected past experiences with present expressions, seeking universal truths.
    • I discovered the moments where poetry can heal and inspire change.
    • I compiled some ideas for solving some of the issues.
  • Tailoring Every Verse
    While I began with broad ideas of poetic themes, I understood that true personalization lies in creating poems that speak to the individual reader. I researched how every reader’s heart beats differently, how each person responds to beauty, pain, love, and loss. I crafted my words, not for the masses, but for the soul of each reader.

    But how can poetry transform a single heart?

    Through careful introspection and collaboration with other poets, I crafted poems that were not just for reading—but for feeling, connecting deeply to one’s identity, thoughts, and dreams.
    The answer lies in the subtleties of words, the pauses between stanzas, and the echoes of universal truths hidden within.
DESIGN PHASE
After absorbing the insights from my poetic exploration, I began shaping the experience from scratch. I transformed abstract thoughts into tangible forms, starting with simple scribbles and evolving into powerful verses that would resonate long after they were read.
TAKEAWAY
In the art of poetry, words are not just letters—they are threads that weave emotions, painting landscapes of the mind. Each poem, a universe unto itself, carries within it the pulse of the writer's heart and the whispers of the world around us. The beauty lies not only in what is said, but in what is left unsaid, in the space between the lines.
